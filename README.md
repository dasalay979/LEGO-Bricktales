## What Is LEGO Bricktales?

LEGO Bricktales is a puzzle-adventure game built around creative problem-solving.

Unlike traditional LEGO games that emphasize combat and collecting, Bricktales encourages players to build functional structures using a limited selection of LEGO pieces.

The game was developed by ClockStone Studio, the creators of the popular Bridge Constructor series, and released on multiple platforms, including macOS.

The result is a refreshing take on LEGO gaming that focuses on imagination, engineering, and experimentation.

## The Story: Saving Grandfather's Amusement Park

The adventure begins with a simple yet heartwarming premise.

Your grandfather owns an aging amusement park that's on the verge of being shut down due to safety concerns and poor maintenance. Accompanied by your tiny robot companion, you embark on a journey across various LEGO worlds to collect Happiness Crystals.

These crystals are needed to restore the amusement park and bring joy back to visitors.

The narrative isn't particularly deep, but it serves its purpose well by providing motivation and connecting the game's diverse environments.

Throughout the journey, you'll encounter colorful LEGO characters who need your help solving problems.

These tasks naturally lead into the game's creative building challenges.
